# Gamma-Gamma Coincidence Ambiguity

## Question

How can gamma-gamma coincidence measurements lead to an incorrect nuclear level scheme?

## Discussion

Coincidence measurements identify cascades of gamma transitions.

However several factors may introduce ambiguity:

• contaminant gamma rays  
• unresolved peaks in the spectrum  
• accidental coincidences  
• overlapping transitions from different nuclei  

These factors may lead to incorrect placement of gamma rays in the level scheme.

## Insight

Careful gating, background subtraction, and comparison with theoretical models are required to avoid misinterpretation.
