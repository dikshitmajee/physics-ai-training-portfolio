
# Electric Field of a Point Charge

## Question
A charge of 5 μC is placed in vacuum. Find the electric field at 2 m distance.

## Formula
E = kq / r²

## Solution
E = (8.99 × 10⁹ × 5 × 10⁻⁶) / (2²)

E ≈ 1.12 × 10⁴ N/C

## Explanation
Electric field decreases with the square of the distance from the charge due to Coulomb's law.
